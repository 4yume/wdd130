Beginer Solo Rafting

Experience a relaxed introduction to rafting for first-timers. During this session, you'll learn the basik skill before heading onto calm river.

  • Skills Level: Beginner
  • Duration: 1.5-2 hours
  • Type: Single-person inflatable raft

Intermediate Rafting

Rapid area rafting experience is designed for people seeking a more dynamic and fast-paced river environment.

  • Skills Level: Intermediate
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Type: Single or shared raft

Family & Group Rafting

This is designed for people of mixed ages and skill levels who wish to enjoy a shared rafting adventure. The river current remains gentle, allowing participants to enjoy a comfortable and moderate pace.

  • Skils Level: Beginner-friendly
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Type: Multi-person raft
